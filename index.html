A 2-DAY NATIONAL LEVEL TECH FEST

ABOUT

IGNITION is a two day National Level Student Tech Fest organised by the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology and Sciences (ANITS) and is conducted by AMEA (Anits Mechanical Engineers Association). " IGNITION 2K24" is a platform for innovators to share their wonderful thoughts in advancement and development of technologies through events like paper presentation, model presentation, robotics, workshop, etc.
